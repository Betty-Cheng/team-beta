How to Help







1. Educate Yourself and Others






Our inability to relate to homelessness is our biggest obstacle to ending it.
The first step in fighting homelessness is getting informed so that we can break down the stereotypes that divide us.














2.community Engagement






Go volunteer at your local organization that helps homeless youth

3. Contact Your Legislators.






Your voice can help end homelessness. Contact your Legislators. Put in your email and zip code and click submit. Tweet, call and write to your federal and state Legislators. Telling them that ending Homelessness is a priority
